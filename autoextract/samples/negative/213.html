FAQ

WHO AM I BUYING FROM?

Weldfabulous is an online superstore for all welding and industrial supply needs. We handle a wide selection of high quality, name-brand items with a local service character.  Learn more about Weldfabulous by clicking here.

 

CAN I PLACE AN ORDER BY TELEPHONE?

Yes, you may place your order by telephone with one of our customer service representatives. 

 

WHAT IF I NEVER RECEIVED AN EMAIL CONFIRMATION?

Click here to access your account. Once logged in, you can print a copy of our confirmation/receipt. Please also check your spam or junk file for any email communication from Weldfabulous. If you have completed the above and still have not received a confirmation your payment method may not have processed correctly.

 

DO YOU ACCEPT INTERNATIONAL ORDERS?

Yes, Weldfabulous does ship internationally with a few exceptions. All international orders must be placed online. Please note, that you, the buyer are responsible for any VAT, tariff, duty taxes, handling fees, customs clearance charges, etc. required by your country for importing customer goods. (No order will be marked with "gift") If you elect not to pay these charges at the time of checkout you will be subject to paying these at the time of delivery. If you refuse to do so Weldfabulous has the right to retrieve the package from the carrier at your expense and return the items to Weldfabulous. Once the items are received back at the Weldfabulous facility the refund will be processed less the retrieval fee and outgoing shipping charges incurred with your purchase. Please do your research so you're aware of the charges being incurred prior to receiving these items into your country.

 

DO YOU SELL GIFT CERTIFICATES?

Yes, gift certificates are available in any denomination between $1.00 - $100.00 and may be purchased and redeemed online only. Gift certificate codes are delivered only by email and will be sent to your recipient immediately upon completion of your order.  Order now by clicking here.

 

DO I NEED TO SIGN FOR MY DELIVERY?

Weldfabulous requires a signature on full cylinders as well as orders with a total value exceeding $500.

 

WILL I BE CHARGED SALES TAX?

Sales tax for U.S. customers is charged to Minnesota, Wisconsin, and Iowa shipping addresses only.

 

WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DOES WELDFABULOUS ACCEPT?

Online payment may be made by Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, or PayPal. Weldfabulous only accepts U.S. billed credit cards on orders placed over the phone. International credit cards can be used through PayPal to make your online purchases.  

Orders may be paid in advance by submitting a wire transfer for the full amount of your order.

 

WHY SHOULD I NOT USE A DEBIT CARD?

When using a debit or credit card, there will be a hold put on your account by your financial institution (not Weldfabulous) for the amount charged for 1-5 business days. The hold will be put on your account regardless of whether the transaction is completed or declined by your financial institution. Weldfabulous is not responsible for overdraft fees or any debit card related charges.

 

CAN I FINANCE MY PURCHASE THROUGH WELDFABULOUS?

No, Weldfabulous does not offer any financing or deferred payment plans. All orders must be paid in full prior to processing. However, you can apply for PayPal Credit.

 

WHAT IF MY ORDER IS DAMAGED?

All packages containing external damage must be refused and Weldfabulous must be contacted immediately. Internal damage must be reported to Weldfabulous within 10 days of receipt. Damage claims will be declined if the item has been used, is not in the original packaging, or was received more than 10 days earlier.
